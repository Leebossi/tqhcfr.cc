Bi-weekly social ride

We organize a bi-weekly social ride in Turku. The ride is open to everyone and we welcome riders of all skill levels. We usually meet at the city center and ride to a nearby cafe or park. Follow us on Instagram for updates on the next ride.

Join the discussion on Yksivaihde.net forum or our Signal chat to stay updated on the next ride and connect with other riders.

End of Days

End of Days is an annual whole day event in an alleycat style race. The race usually takes place in May and the route is revealed at the start of the race. The entire race distance is usually around 100-200 km and the route includes various checkpoints around the province. However the it's up to every rider themself how long they desire to ride. The event is open to everyone and we welcome riders of all skill levels.

The race has gained a lot of popularity over the years and in 2025 we had 42 riders at the start line.

In 2026 the race will take place on May 23rd in the city of Hämeenlinna.

Join the discussion on Yksivaihde.net forum to vibe on past years events, to stay updated on the next race and connect with other riders.
